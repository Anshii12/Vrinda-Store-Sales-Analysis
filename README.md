# Vrinda Store Annual Report 2022 | Sales Analysis

## Project Overview
This project involves a comprehensive analysis of **Vrinda Store's** 2022 sales data to uncover key customer trends, regional performance, and channel efficiency. The goal is to provide data-driven insights that can predict and drive future sales growth.

---

## Key Insights for 2023 Growth

### 1. Customer Persona & Demographics
*   **The Power Buyer:** **Middle-aged Women** are the most valuable segment, contributing over **$8.16M** in revenue.
*   **Gender Split:** Across all age groups, **Women (64%)** significantly outspend Men (36%).
*   **Action:** Marketing campaigns should be tailored to the preferences and shopping habits of the 30-49 age demographic.

### 2. Seasonal Trends
*   **March Madness:** Sales peaked in **March**, likely due to seasonal promotions or holidays. 
*   **Action:** Analyze the "March strategy" to replicate similar success in the Q4 (Oct-Dec) period where sales showed a slight decline.

### 3. Geographical Performance
*   **Top 3 States:** **Maharashtra, Karnataka, and Uttar Pradesh** drive the bulk of the revenue.
*   **Action:** Focus logistics and localized advertising budget on these three states to maximize ROI.

### 4. Channel & Operations
*   **Marketplace Dominance:** **Amazon (35%)** and **Flipkart (22%)** are the primary sales drivers.
*   **Order Health:** Maintained a strong **92% Delivery Rate**.
*   **Action:** Investigate the **6% combined Returns/Cancellations** to identify if specific product categories or regions are underperforming.

---

## Data Analysis Techniques Used
*   **Data Cleaning:** Handling null values and formatting currency for consistency.
*   **Pivot Tables:** Used to calculate `% of Row Total` and `% of Grand Total` for demographic splits.
*   **Visualizations:** 
    *   **100% Stacked Bar Charts** for gender distribution.
    *   **Donut Charts** for channel contribution.
    *   **Line & Clustered Column Charts** for month-over-month sales trends.
*   **Forecasting:** Linear trendline analysis to predict future "increment sales."
